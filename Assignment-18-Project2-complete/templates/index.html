Where do the world's top men's teams and players come from?

With the women's World Cup in full swing along with regional men's cups being played, we recalled the U.S. women's team consistently doing well while the U.S. men's team usually performing poorly in the World Cup. This led us to ask a few questions, primarily, "Who are the world's top men's team players and where do they come from?"
